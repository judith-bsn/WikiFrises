What is WikiFrises?

WikiFrises is a website that gathers thousands of timelines on various subjects. These timelines are free to download for personal or educational purposes. The content of the site is based on the Wiki principle, which allows the collaborative creation, modification and illustration of pages within the website. Thus, we want to check each of our sources before validating the publication of a timeline and we invite you to share your knowledge on any subject...
